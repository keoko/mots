Welcome to Mots! 👋

🎯 What is Mots?

A fun way to learn English vocabulary for FAR students. Translate Catalan words to English with Wordle-style gameplay!

🎨 How it works

Guess the English word and get color feedback:

H
Green = Correct position
O
Orange = Wrong position
U
Gray = Not in word

💡 Tip: Click the ❓ button anytime for help!

How to Play

🎯 Goal

Translate Catalan words to English. You have 5 attempts per word.

🎮 Game Modes

📖 Study Mode: Review all words before playing

🎮 Play Mode: Jump straight in and test yourself

🎨 Color Guide

H
Correct letter, correct position
O
Correct letter, wrong position
U
Letter not in word

💡 Tips

  • Click the 🎮 Mots title to return home anytime
  • Progress is saved automatically
  • Works completely offline after first visit
